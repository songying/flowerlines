Description (4000 chars max)

Flower Lines is a relaxing yet strategic puzzle game inspired by the classic Lines 98. Place colorful flowers on a 9×9 grid, plan your moves carefully, and line up 5 or more flowers of the same color to score.

Easy to learn. Impossible to put down.

HOW TO PLAY
• Tap a flower to select it
• Tap an empty cell to move it — flowers can only travel along open paths
• Line up 5 or more flowers of the same color (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) to clear them and score
• After each move, 3 new flowers bloom — so think ahead!
• The game ends when the board fills up. Beat your best score!

FEATURES
✿ 7 beautifully hand-drawn flower types in vivid colors
✿ Classic Lines 98 gameplay reimagined with a floral theme
✿ Smooth animations — flowers slide, bloom, and burst
✿ Score tracking with personal best
✿ Soothing background music with independent BGM & SFX volume controls
✿ Works great on iPhone and iPad

TIPS FOR HIGH SCORES
→ Always clear lines before moving flowers that aren't in danger
→ Keep the center of the board open as long as possible
→ Watch the 3 upcoming flowers — plan around them!

Free to play. No time limits. No lives. Just pure puzzle strategy at your own pace.
